What this study cannot prove

A careful project is clear about where its answer stops.

Not a living-cell test

The models do not show what happens in bacteria, animals, or people.

Not drug advice

The energy numbers do not say which treatment a person should receive.

Not binding free energy

The compact interaction-energy proxy leaves out important motion, solvent, and environment effects.

Not quantum advantage

The completed molecular quantum result is from local emulation, not physical quantum hardware.

Still missing: matching finished quantum-energy results for all four systems, a verified result from the current remote retry, physical-hardware molecular energy, and laboratory validation.
