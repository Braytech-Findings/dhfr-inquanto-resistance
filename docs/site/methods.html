How the study works

We make small computer models, calculate their energy, and check whether repeated quantum-style measurements give a steady answer.

1

Build

Prepare WT and L28R protein structures with TMP and 4-DTMP.

2

Calculate

Run the same compact classical method for all four models.

3

Measure

Turn the validated WT_TMP electronic problem into 576 groups of quantum measurements.

4

Check

Compare with the exact answer and report uncertainty instead of hiding it.

Technical recipe

The validated WT_TMP estimator uses STO-3G, six selected spatial orbitals, 12 qubits, 117 saved UCCSD parameters, and 1,819 Pauli terms grouped into 576 circuits. Local convergence used three replicates at seven shot levels from 100 through 10,000 shots per circuit.

For every command and scientific assumption, read the reproducibility guide.
