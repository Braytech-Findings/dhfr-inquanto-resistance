# Regeneron STS 2027 compliance checklist

Official sources: [2027 application requirements](https://www.societyforscience.org/regeneron-sts/application-requirements/),
[official rules](https://www.societyforscience.org/regeneron-sts/official-rules/),
and [official FAQ](https://www.societyforscience.org/regeneron-sts/frequently-asked-questions/).

- [ ] Student confirms individual-project eligibility.
- [ ] Student and mentor verify all required permissions and Task 7 paperwork.
- [ ] Student completes the Ethics Statement attestation.
- [ ] All paid support, outside support, pre-existing relationships, and AI use
      are disclosed accurately.
- [ ] Student independently writes the Research Report, abstract, application
      answers, conclusions, bibliography, and citations without generative AI.
- [ ] Research Report is no more than 20 content pages and follows the current
      official formatting rules.
- [ ] Every figure, graph, table, and image has a student-written full citation
      directly underneath it, following the official citation guide.
- [ ] Student verifies every number against the machine-readable source file.
- [ ] Student confirms that pilot-only, missing, simulated, and emulated evidence
      is labeled correctly.
- [ ] Student submits before **Thursday, November 5, 2026 at 8:00 PM Eastern**;
      adult recommendations are also due then.

This checklist is administrative support, not application or Research Report
content.
