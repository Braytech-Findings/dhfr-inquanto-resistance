# Glossary

| Term | Simple meaning | Technical meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Active space | The small part studied closely | Selected molecular orbitals treated explicitly in correlated calculation |
| Ansatz | A flexible answer pattern | Parameterized trial quantum state |
| Emulator | A computer imitation | Classical simulation of a target quantum backend |
| Hamiltonian | Energy rulebook | Operator defining a quantum system’s energy |
| Hartree–Fock | A first energy estimate | Mean-field electronic-structure method |
| Orbital | An electron pattern | One-electron wavefunction/basis state |
| Pauli operator | A measurement direction | Tensor product of I, X, Y, Z qubit operators |
| Qubit | Quantum information unit | Two-level quantum degree of freedom |
| Shot | One repeated observation | One sampled circuit execution |
| UCCSD | Circuit recipe | Unitary coupled-cluster singles-and-doubles ansatz |
| VQE | Energy-search method | Variational quantum eigensolver |
| Uncertainty | How much a result may vary | Here, reported standard error from finite sampling |
